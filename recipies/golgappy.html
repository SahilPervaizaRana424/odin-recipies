GolGappas

Ingredients

  • Moong dal
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Oil

Instructions

  1. Soak the moong dal in water for 2-3 hours.
  2. Drain the water and grind the moong dal into a smooth paste.
  3. Add salt to the paste and mix well.
  4. Heat oil in a deep frying pan.
  5. Take small portions of the paste and shape them into small balls.
  6. Flatten the balls into discs and fry them in hot oil until they puff up and turn golden brown.
  7. Remove the fried discs from the oil and drain
