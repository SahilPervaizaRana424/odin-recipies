Zarda

Ingredients

  • Basmati rice
  • Sugar
  • Food color
  • Cardamom
  • Raisins
  • Almonds
  • Cashews
  • Ghee

Instructions

  1. Wash and boil the rice.
  2. Drain the rice and keep it aside.
  3. Heat ghee in a pan.
  4. Add sugar, cardamom, and food color.
  5. Mix the boiled rice with the sweet mixture.
  6. Add raisins, almonds, and cashews.
  7. Cook on low heat for 10 minutes and serve.
