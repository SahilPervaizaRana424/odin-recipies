Biryani

Ingredients

  • Basmati rice
  • Chicken
  • Yogurt
  • Onion
  • Tomatoes
  • Biryani masala
  • Oil
  • Salt

Instructions

  1. Wash and soak the rice.
  2. Fry the onions until golden brown.
  3. Cook the chicken with tomatoes, yogurt, and biryani masala.
  4. Boil the rice until it is almost cooked.
  5. Layer the rice and chicken mixture.
  6. Cook on low heat for 20 minutes.
  7. Serve hot with raita.
