Channa Chaat

Ingredients

  • Boiled chickpeas
  • Onion
  • Tomato
  • Green chili
  • Lemon juice
  • Chaat masala
  • Salt
  • Coriander leaves

Instructions

  1. Wash and boil the chickpeas.
  2. Chop the onion, tomato, and green chili.
  3. Add the vegetables to the chickpeas.
  4. Add salt and chaat masala.
  5. Squeeze fresh lemon juice.
  6. Mix everything well.
  7. Garnish with coriander leaves and serve.
